Mix ins are some of the more powerful elements of Sass. They are analogs of functions in programming languages. A mix in is a fragment of Sass that can easily be applied to another selector. Mix ins can include arguments that allow you to vary your values. To define a mix in, all you need to type is @mixin, followed by the name of the mix in and then its styling. When you want to use the mix in, just type @include Itâ€™s useful to have mix ins in a separate style sheet, keeping your main style sheet cleaner.
